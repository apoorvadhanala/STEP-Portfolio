PHOTOGRAPHY

WHY I LOVE TAKING PICTURES

From a young age, I’ve loved taking pictures and in fact, used to take so many that I’d be told to put my phone away at times. I later got my first professional, DSLR camera as a high school graduation present and from then, taking pictures turned into something more than just something I enjoyed: it turned into a passion and hobby. I love taking pictures for any occasion from senior and graduation pictures to portrait sessions, family events, and traveling; I especially enjoy discovering new places and capturing the essence of what makes that place beautiful. For me, a picture is more than a picture - it is a memory and a way to be transported back to a particular place or time.
